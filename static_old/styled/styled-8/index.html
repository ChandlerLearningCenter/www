We now offer SKYPE tutoring. A few years ago, even a few months ago, Skype tutoring would have been inferior to our in-Center experience. Since the advances in Internet speed, technology, software, and hardware, we are now able to confidently offer our services over the Internet.

Today, each of our tutors are equipped with 27 inch iMac computers with the latest version of Skype. We have created (and are constantly updating) multimedia content that we currently use in sessions and in our classes to better enhance the learning experience. This is especially useful in our Skype sessions, as we are able to enable screen sharing capabilities. What this means is that we are not simply talking at you whilst gesticulating, but the experience is as fluid as an in-person session.

Skype tutoring is a great option if you:
  • are at boarding school / college
  • travel a lot and are away for the weekends/summer
  • can't get to the Center or want to cut down on travel/drive time
  • have a last minute / emergency need!
  • live in another country (yes, we currently tutor studentswho live in foreign countries!)

Contact us to schedule a Skype session or ask us questions about it!
